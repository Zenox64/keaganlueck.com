Media Study

Project 3 The media I studied was print making. I had done this in the past and I wanted to refresh my experiences. When I heard the word print I automatically though of money printing. That is where the inspiration from this project came, a trigger from the word print. I feel like the United States government has printed way too much money in the past years and have failed the American citizens. That is why I created a print of $11 bills, they are nonexistent(as of now). An $11 bill would be ridicoulous, just like printing a monstrosity of US currency. I used that ridicoulousness to try and get my point across. I do not think that other people would really understand my work unless I explained it to them.
